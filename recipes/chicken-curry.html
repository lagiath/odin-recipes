Thai Green Chicken Curry

Description

Don't call the local takeaway â€“ impress friends and family by cooking this fragrant and creamy Thai green chicken curry. It's easy and quick to make.

Green curry is thick, creamy, filling, and bright. Between red, yellow, or green, the latter is one of the most famous and sought-after dishes, beautifully vibrant thanks to the addition of Thai basil, cilantro, and makrut lime leaf and peel.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) SunRice Long Grain Jasmine Rice
  • 2 tbsp Alfa One Rice Bran Oil
  • 1 brown onion, cut into thin wedges
  • 1/2 x 210g jar Valcom Authentic Thai Green Curry Paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 300ml chicken stock
  • 450g Coles RSPCA Approved Chicken Breast Fillets, cut into 1cm-thick strips
  • 1 green capsicum, seeded, thinly sliced
  • 100g snow peas, trimmed (optional)
  • 1/2 bunch coriander, leaves picked
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup (65g) bean sprouts (optional)
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Cook the rice following the packet directions.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the onion and cook, stirring, for 2 mins or until onion softens.
  4. Add the curry paste. Cook, stirring, for 2 mins or until fragrant.
  5. Add the coconut milk and stock and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the chicken, capsicum and snow peas, if desired, and cook for 2-3 mins or until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Chop half the coriander leaves and add to the curry.
  8. Stir in the fish sauce, sugar and half the bean sprouts, if desired.
  9. Squeeze over half the lime wedges. Taste and add more fish sauce, sugar and lime juice, if desired.
